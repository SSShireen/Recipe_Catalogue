Chicken Caesar Salad

Servings: 4

Time Taken: 20 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 Chicken Breast piece
  • 15 grams Breadcrumbs
  • 1 tsp Paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 5 grams Flour
  • 1 Egg
  • 10 grams Parmesan Cheese

    • Salad Dressing:

  • Lettuce
  • Green Peppers
  • 2 tbsp Mayonnaise
  • Garlic Clove

Method:

  1. Butterfly the chicken and place in the flour
  2. Shake well and dip into the egg
  3. Place into breadcrumbs with garlic and paprika
  4. Spray with oil and bake for 180 degrees celcius at 20 - 25 minutes
  5. Mix Salad dressing together and toss lettuse in it
  6. Add salad dressing to a plate and place green peppers on it
  7. slice the chicken and place on top
