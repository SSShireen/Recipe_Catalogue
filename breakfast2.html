Overnight Oats

Servings: 4

Time Taken: 20 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup Oats
  • 1/2 cup Milk
  • 2 tablespoon Yogurt
  • 1/2 tablespoon Chia Seeds

Method:

  1. In a glass jar with a lid put Oats, Milk and Chia Seeds.
  2. On top put Yogurt.
  3. Add cut up bit - sized Strawberries and Banana or any other fruit you want.
  4. You can drizzle some Honey on top.
