Sandwich

Servings: 4

Time Taken: 20 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 Medium Diced Onion
  • 2 Chicken Breast Pieces
  • 1 1/2 cup Mayonnaise
  • 1/4 Heavy Cream
  • Spices (as per taste)
  • 1/2 cup Shredded Cheese
  • Slices of Bread

Method:

  1. In a pot of boiling water cut Chicken into three pieces and alowly put it in the water
  2. Let chicken cook throughly and then remove from water.
  3. Shred Chicken using two forks until it looks like strings.
  4. Add Chicken to a bowl with Mayonnaise, Heavy Cream, Cheese, Spices and Onion.
  5. Mix throughly and spread on bread and Sandwich press.
