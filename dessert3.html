Blondies

Servings: 4

Time Taken: 40 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 cup Brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup White sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla
  • 2 egg
  • 1/2 teaspoonsalt
  • 1 1/2 cup All Purpose Flour
  • 1 cup Chocolate Chips

Method:

  1. In a bowl mix Butter, Brown Sugar and White Sugar and whisk.
  2. Add in Eggs and Vanilla and mix.
  3. Mix in All Purpose Flour and Salt.
  4. Add Chocolate chips and mix.
  5. Pour in a baking tin with butter and Parchment paper.
  6. You can add a few Chocolate chips on top.
  7. Bake for 25 - 30 min at 180 degrees Celcius.
