Oreo Truffle Cups

Servings: 4

Time Taken: 20 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 25 Oreo
  • 250 ml of Heavy Cream
  • 5 tsp Butter
  • 100G Chocolate Chips

Method:

  1. Blend 25 Oreos until turned to powder.
  2. Put in a bowl and add Butter and mix.
  3. Put in a flat dish and press down.
  4. Whisk Heavy Crean until stiff peaks form.
  5. Mix in crushed Oreos and mix.
  6. Layer on top of the crush Oreo.
  7. Melt Chocolate Chips and pour on top.
