Pasta

Servings: 4

Time Taken: 20 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 1 medium onion - diced
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste

    • Spices Mix

  • Salt
  • pepper
  • onion powder
  • garlic powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • Paprika
  • 1 cup Heavy whipping Cream
  • grated cheese

Method:

  1. In a pot put your pasta along with water let pasta cook
  2. In a hot pan add butter and diced onions and saute
  3. Add tomato paste and mix until combined
  4. Add Spices and garlic paste and mix for 2 minutes
  5. Add Heavy whipping Cream with water and mix until you get an orange colour
  6. Add in cheese in low flame and mix
  7. Add pasta once it is cooke and mix everything together and let simmer for 2 - 3 minutes and serve
