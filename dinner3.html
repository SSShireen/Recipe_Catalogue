French-Style Chicken And Potatoes

Servings: 4

Time Taken: 20 minutes

Ingredients:

    Condensed cream of chicken soup

  • 20g unsalted butter
  • 2 tbsp plain flour
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • 1/2 cup Massel chicken style liquid stock
  • Large pinch of onion powder
  • Large pinch of garlic powder
  • Large pinch of dried thyme
  • Large pinch of salt

    • French-style chicken and potatoes

  • 2 tsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 4 Coles RSPCA Approved Chicken Thigh Cutlets
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 brown onion, sliced
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1 cup Massel chicken style liquid stock
  • 1/4 cup dijon mustard
  • 2 tsp white sugar
  • 3 carrots, cut into thick batons
  • 500g baby red delight potatoes, halved
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme, plus extra to serve

Method:

  1. Make condensed chicken soup: Melt butter in a small saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Add flour. Stir to create a paste. Cook for 1 minute.
  3. Whisk in milk until mixture forms a smooth and thick paste.
  4. Gradually whisk in stock, onion powder, garlic powder, thyme and salt until mixture is smooth.
  5. Cook, whisking constantly, for 2 to 3 minutes or until mixture thickens. Remove from heat.
  6. Set aside to cool 10 minutes.
  7. Preheat oven to 200C/180C fan-forced.
  8. Meanwhile, heat oil in a large heavy-based flameproof ovenproof dish over high heat.
  9. Add chicken, skin-side down. Cook for 3 minutes or until skin is light golden. Turn.
  10. Cook for 2 minutes. Transfer to a plate.
  11. Carefully drain any excess fat from dish. Return to heat.
  12. Add garlic and onion. Cook, stirring, for 2 minutes.
  13. Simmer, stirring, for 3 minutes.
  14. Add Condensed Chicken Soup, the water, stock, mustard and sugar.
  15. Stir to dissolve mustard. Add carrot, potato, rosemary and thyme.
  16. Stir to combine.
  17. Return chicken to pan. Cover. Transfer to oven. Bake for 30 minutes.
  18. Remove lid. Bake for a further 20 minutes or until chicken is dark brown and cooked through.
  19. Stand 5 minutes. Serve sprinkled with extra thyme.
