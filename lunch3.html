Salmon Rice Bowl

Servings: 4

Time Taken: 20 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 Salmon Piece
  • 4 Cherry tomato
  • 1/4 corn
  • I Serve Rice

Method:

  1. Rub salt and pepper onto Salmon
  2. Pan - fry Salmon starting with the skin and sear unitl each side is golden brown
  3. In a plate put your serving of rice, cut up cherry tomatos in half, place corn on the side and salmon piece on top
