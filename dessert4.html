Churros

Servings: 4

Time Taken: 20 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 egg

Method:

  1. In a pan add Water, Butter, Sugar and a pinch of salt and mix until melted.
  2. Add flour and mix until it forms a ball.
  3. let cool for 3 - 5 minutes.
  4. Add egg and mix.
  5. use a pipign bag with a star - shaped wand for crisp edges.
  6. fill piping bag with dough.
  7. on a piece of parchment paper pipe the desired lenght.
  8. deep fry over high heat until golden brown.
  9. while still hot roll in a 1:1 ratio of sugar and cinnamon powder.
  10. for the dipping sauce melt chocolate chips or nutella in the microwave.
