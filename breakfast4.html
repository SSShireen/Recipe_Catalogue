French Toast

Servings: 4

Time Taken: 20 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 cup Milk
  • 1 tsp Cinnamon Powder
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Essense
  • 8 slices of Brioche Bread

Method:

  1. Crack Eggs in a bowl and whisk together.
  2. Add Milk and mix with the Eggs.
  3. Add Cinnamon and Vanilla Essense.
  4. Dip the Bread in the Egg mixture and put on a medium heat pan with Butter.
  5. You can add Fruits, Golden Syrup and Powdered Sugar to your French Toast.
